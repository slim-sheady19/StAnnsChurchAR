## Installation
NOTE: the app only works for Android devices at this time.

Go to [this link](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1FXYjYD34zkY2hYF__Uu-vzqaNJOQD0Do/view?usp=sharing) and download StAnns_11.19.apk 

Install on device.  For help with this step see this short video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N0M4XGkpCn4

Go to one of the optimal viewing angles marked by the red squares on the map below (**WARNING:** Please be aware of your surroundings when on location, it is a busy Montreal intersection!)
![image](https://github.com/slim-sheady19/StAnnsChurchAR/assets/58230539/a42b427a-e792-48ed-8bbe-29c35ae7916c)

Launch the app, press start. When the camera is showing on the screen, you might need to move the camera around your position because the church is positioned based on what the camera is seeing.

If the church loses its positioning, force close the app by swiping it up and relaunching.
